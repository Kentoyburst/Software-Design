Review Comments

This thesis presents an excellent analysis of urban planning solutions for traffic congestion. The methodology is well-structured and the data collection was thorough. The literature review covers most key aspects but could benefit from more recent international case studies. The analysis of traffic patterns is insightful and the proposed solutions are practical. The writing quality is exceptional with clear organization and professional academic presentation.

Recommended for approval with minor revisions to expand on comparative international case studies in the literature review section.
