Development of Machine Learning Algorithm for Predicting Student Performance

Authors: John Smith

Department: Computer Science

Submitted: May 5, 2025

Abstract

This research aims to develop a machine learning algorithm that can predict student academic performance based on various factors including attendance, participation, previous grades, and socioeconomic factors. The study utilizes neural networks and decision trees to create a predictive model with over 85% accuracy. The findings demonstrate significant correlations between certain behavioral patterns and academic outcomes, which could be utilized by educational institutions to implement early intervention strategies for at-risk students.
